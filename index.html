Manifesto
For 200,000 years, death was not a problem to be solved. It was simply the condition of being alive. That assumption is being revised — in laboratories, in clinical trials, in the data coming back from the first generation of interventions that treat ageing as a biological process rather than an inevitability.
The science has crossed a threshold. The question now is whether you are paying attention. Billions in research capital. Thousands of scientists. Convergent technologies arriving simultaneously — AI-accelerated drug discovery, epigenetic reprogramming, cellular rejuvenation. The timeline is compressing faster than public awareness.
